Why Analyze the Tongue?

The tongue acts as a mirror to your internal health. Medical experts and traditional practitioners alike use tongue analysis to detect early signs of digestive issues. Changes in its color, texture, coating, and moisture can hint at problems in your digestive tract such as indigestion, nutrient malabsorption, acidity, or gut imbalances.
